**Healthy Meal**

An application to track your calories, save your meals and see what you are eating everyday!

First, create an account and log in, then type the food you want in the Foods tab and choose to add it at the bottom of the page. Food data is fetched from USDA Food API. 
You can log out anytime, and view all your meals in the My Plan section once you click "Filter" or specify the meal you added. 

Planning to add more functionality in the future, but this is a basic HTML/CSS + Django/PostgreSQL-run application
